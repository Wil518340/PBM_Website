About us

Pris Banh Mi
in Columbus

Paris Banh Mi Cafe Bakery in Dublin, OH, is a unique Vietnamese sandwich and coffee shop offering traditional banh mi, fresh-baked bread, and a variety of handcrafted specialty drinks, including the famous Paris By Night. They also serve slush, milk, and fresh tea made to order, along with a daily selection of French bakery items, cakes, and pastries. Committed to exceptional customer service and food safety, they use the freshest ingredients and maintain clean workstations for an unforgettable dining experience with top-notch quality and great value. Explore their freshly prepared items to delight your taste buds.
